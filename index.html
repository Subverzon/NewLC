About Us

LiveCraft is an Esport Organisation which believe in bringing up change in an Esport World. Our mission is to bring Esports into every household.

Our Events

Prestige Cup

Prestige Cup was a PUBG Mobile tournament organized by LiveCraft. A total of 230 teams registered for this tournament out of which 10 best Semi-Pro teams were qualified for the final battle against the top elite teams of India which consists of Team Soul, Entity, Team XsparK, 8Bit, Mayhem and many more. Our mission was to provide light to Semi-Pro teams and Prestige Cup was our first step towards it.

Prestige Cup 2.0

Prestige Cup 2.0 was a PUBG Mobile tournament organized by LiveCraft powered by Insidious Esports and had a prize pool of Rs.15,000. Prestige Cup 2.0, version changed but not out motive, our focus was still the same to provide Light to Semi – Pro teams. Starting from October 20 to 27, 240 Semi-Pro teams were battling against each other to pick out 10 teams for the Grand Finals. After that 22 elite teams had battle in Elite Semi-Finals for the Top 5, and 7 slots were reserved for Top Elite Teams.
