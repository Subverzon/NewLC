About Us

LiveCraft is an Esport Organisation which believe in bringing up change in an Esport World. The name LiveCraft is represented by two characters, the top character, "Live" means something which is real or alive, while the bottom character, "Craft" an activity involving skills. Our mission is to bring Esports into every household.
